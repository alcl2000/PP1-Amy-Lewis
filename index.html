About Us

Frost's is a family run bakery in Canterbury, located on the historic High Street. We sell A variety of delicious treats for everyone to enjoy. We have options for delivery and collection throughout the CT2 area, and we're more than happy to take orders for parties!

We know you have a lot of choice in Bakeries in Canterbury, so why choose us? We only use the freshest ingredients, for the heighest quality.
